Contact

Want to get in touch with server administrators? Here's how.

Modmail

This is the preferred method. If you've joined the server, navigate to the server's sidebar on the right-hand side and locate the ModMail user. When you send that user a direct message, we'll receive it.

Alternatives

If you can't DM that bot for whatever reason, there's a few alternatives;
You can ping an online moderator or administrator in chat. If you can't join the server or use that method, send an email to waffles@iphonediscord.info with your inquiry. Do not use this for ban appeals. To appeal a ban, navigate to the appeals page.
