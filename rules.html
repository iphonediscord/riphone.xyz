Server Rules

All users are expected to read and understand the rules before participating. If you have any questions, please use the modmail bot hoisted in the server's sidebar. Rules are subject to change. Staff reserve the right to take corrective action for reasons not listed. By participating in the server, you are agreeing to the rules listed below.

Personal Attacks and General Conduct

No personal attacks, harassment, sexism, racism, or general malicious behavior. Do not spam or be obnoxious, annoying, or otherwise disturbing to other members.

Political Discussion & Commentary

Political discussion is limited to our off-topic channel. Sexism, racism, and the like are strictly prohibited. Violation of this rule will result in an instant mute, kick, or ban.

NSFW Content

NSFW content is not permitted on this server, as per Discord Partner Code of Conduct. Violation of this rule will result in removal from the server.

Activation Lock Support

Discussion involving the bypass of Apple's Activation Lock or Passcode Lock is strictly prohibited. Please contact your local Apple Store for assistance.

Self Promotion

Self-promotion including server invites and affiliate links is prohibited. If you'd like your server listed in our affiliates channel, please send your request with info to the modmail bot in the server's sidebar.

Piracy

This server does not tolerate sharing of piracy, as per Discord, Inc. Terms of Service. Offenders will be banned.
