Server Rules

All users are expected to read and understand the rules before participating. If you have any questions, please use the modmail bot hoisted in the server's sidebar. Rules are subject to change. Staff reserve the right to take corrective action for reasons not listed. By participating in the server, you are agreeing to the rules listed below.

Rule 1: Malicious, toxic, and discriminatory behavior

Malicious behavior including but not limited to trolling, provoking, or using discriminatory rhetoric or slurs towards any person or group isn't allowed and will result in swift removal from the community. Repeated toxic behavior may result in removal from the community. Other prohibited behaviors include doxxing, impersonation of users or bots, and recording voice channels or deleted messages without consent.

Rule 2: NSFW content

NSFW (not safe for work) or otherwise inappropriate or disturbing content or conversation is strictly prohibited.

Rule 3: Self-promotion and soliciting

Self-promotion and advertising is prohibited including but not limited to Discord server invite links, affiliate links, and social media channel links. Do not solicit, ask for, buy, sell, or trade products or money.

Rule 4: Political discussion

Political discussion is strictly limited to the #news-and-politics channel. Bigotry disguised as political opinions isn't acceptable.

Rule 5: Illegal activity

Discussion of the commission of crimes or participation in activities that are broadly illegal or immoral isn't allowed here. This includes but is not limited to the discussion or distribution of piracy or pirated content including illegitimate developer beta profiles. Discussion involving the bypass of Apple's Activation Lock or Passcode Lock is strictly prohibited.

Rule 6: Server language

The server's language is English, we ask that you keep conversations to English only.
